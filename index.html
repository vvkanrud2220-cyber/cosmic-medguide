Ayurvedic Treatment Tips

  • Holistic Approach: Ayurveda treats the root cause, not just symptoms. Patience is key as natural remedies take time to show full effect.
  • Dietary Balance: Align your diet with your dosha (body type). Avoid extremely cold or processed foods during treatment.
  • Hydration: Drink warm water regularly to help flush out toxins (Ama) from the body.
  • Consultation: Never mix Ayurvedic treatments with allopathic (general) medicines without consulting both your doctors to avoid harmful interactions.
